About Me

X-ray Astronomy Researcher

I am a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Inter-University Centre for Astronomy and Astrophysics (IUCAA), Pune, specializing in X-ray astronomy and high-energy astrophysics. My research focuses on X-ray binaries, neutron stars, black holes, and X-ray polarization.

My work involves analyzing data from space-based observatories like AstroSAT, XMM-Newton, NuSTAR, and Chandra to understand the physics of compact objects and their environments.
